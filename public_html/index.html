About River State ICT
The Rivers State Information and communication Technology (ICT) department was created in 2009

The Rivers State Information and communication Technology (ICT) Department was created in 2009 to support and empower the transformation of the government and economy of the state by proactive application of Information and Communication Technology for improved public service delivery and e-governance to the People of the state.

Our Unit
River state ICT Unit
01
Administrative unit

This Unit is responsible for the administrative running and activities in the ICT unit.

02
Accounts unit

This Unit is tasked with the management of the units finances, monetary resources, payments, funds, assets and credits.

03
Operations and Networking Unit

This unit is in charge of all the networking infrastructure, networking facilities and networking administration and operations in the MDA’s in the State.

04
Training and Help desk unit

This unit is responsible for training and retraining of all staff, support staff and personnel of all the government workers in various MDA’s. They are also respond to real time request from the various clients in the MDA’s about problems with their ICT infrastructure.

05
Database(biometrics) unit

This unit is responsible for all the management and administration of the database management software used in organizing the bio data of all the staffs of the various MDA in the society.

06
Maintenance and repair unit

This unit is responsible for maintaining of all hardware system, software applications in all the various MDA in the Rivers State . They also are engaged in repair of various ICT facilities and infrastructure in the MDA’s.

07
ICT Training Centre

This unit is responsible for training and retraining of all staff, support staff and personnel of all the government workers in various MDA’s. They are also respond to real time request from the various clients in the MDA’s about problems with their ICT infrastructure.

08
Planning Research & Statistics unit
(Web &Application Development)

Designing, development, deployment and maintaining of Websites for all the MDA’s for Rivers State. Building and developing of apps that can be applied to management information systems for all MDA in the State. Develop and maintain a management Information system for the ICT sector with the Ministry.

09
Media unit

This unit is in charge of information management and dissemination in all the spheres of the ICT unit. The keep pictorial archives of all the day to today activities of the ICT unit.

INITIATIVES(PROJECTS)
Rivers state ICT Initiatives
01
ICT Farm:

This is a project that was design for Children, Teenagers and Youths. This is to improve their computer competency levels and build up their ICT skill.

02
Basic Digital Literacy Programme:

This theme and drive of this programs is to improve computer literacy of the citizens of Rivers State

03
Codegaminators Programme:

This program is design for young programmers. Multiple coding programmes, application software and database courses are taught by highly seasoned lecturers.

04
Court Management Information Systems (Rivcomis)

This is an application software design by the ICT department which is being deployed to the Rivers State High Court for seamless and efficient computerized court procedures.

05
Health Management Information Systems (RivHmis):

This software was developed by the ICT Department ,and deployed to Rivers State Teaching Hospital( formerly BMH). This software helps in the Automation of all the health management processes in the Hospital

06
Public Service Management Information Systems (RivPusmis):

This software is design to build a database management , architectures and structures that will aid in the automation processes of the biometrics exercises for all newly employed, Permanent staff and pensioners in all MDAs of the State

07
Tax Management Information Systems (RivTamis):

This software is developed for the Automation processes for the Rivers State Board of Internal Revenue for comprehensive revenue generation through accurate tax payment

08
Urban Development Management Information System (RivUrban):

This software designed and developed by the department , and was installed in the Ministry of Physical Planning and Urban Development. This make sure every building plan registration is automated.

09
Education Management Information System (RivEmis):

A beautiful software designed and deployed to Ministry of Education for Automation processes in terms of School management operations , School registrations, Results preparations, and certificated Disbursment

Goals And Objectives
River state ICT goals/objectives
01

Create and maintain the enterprise network infrastructure to provide network connectivity and email to all government offices in Rivers State, as well as technical support for all Rivers State Government websites.

02

Provide continuous training and re-orientation of all civic and public servant, ICT interns and Rivers State residents in the adoption and use of information and communication technology.

03

3. Promote efficiency in Government operations through the automation of Government processes, activities and functions using information and communication technology.

04

4. Develop and implement strategies to grow the information and communication technology sector in Rivers State and work with other bodies to promote the adoption and use of information and communication technology.
